About Us

AMC Robotics, the official Robotics Team of ACCEL Middle College, is a student-led organization that enables students to explore STEM pathways through hands-on mechanical and software design and development. Also known as VEX Team 47731M, it was founded in 2020, and we are a dedicated group of students passionate about technology and design. We learn and practice engineering principles and techniques over the course of designing, building, and programming our competition-ready robot. Founded on principles of dedication, innovation, and collaboration, our team is open to all students of ACCEL Middle College (and some East Side Union High School District students), regardless of prior experience.
